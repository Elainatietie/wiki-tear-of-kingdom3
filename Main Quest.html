Main Quest

Tears of the Kingdom features 23 main story quests, which take Link to every corner of the kingdom. This guide will contain a list of each mission, along with its location and who gives it. Players looking to get a sense of Tears of the Kingdom's size, as well as those simply looking to gauge their progress, will find everything they need here.
